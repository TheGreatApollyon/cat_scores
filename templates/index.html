General Rules & Registration Guidelines

General Rules

  • No form of foul words or misconduct is allowed. Teams should consult coordinators if any stunt or costume design may be deemed inappropriate.
  • Teams must inform coordinators about any props they plan to use. Use of water, fire, animal skin, or hazardous substances is strictly prohibited.
  • No religious sentiments or individual interests must be harmed. Violation will lead to direct disqualification.
  • Cross-cluster teams will not be entertained under any circumstances.
  • Decisions made by judges or event coordinators are final and must not be questioned. No form of impropriety will be tolerated.
  • No refunds will be initiated under any circumstance.
  • Students who are part of any performing clubs are not allowed to participate in competitions or events related to their respective club's performance category.

Registration Guidelines

  • Complete the registration form with accurate information
  • Registration fees are non-refundable
  • Participants must carry their college ID during the event
  • Event timings and venues are tentative and subject to change
  • Points will be awarded based on the event category and position

By registering for events, you agree to adhere to all the above rules and guidelines.
